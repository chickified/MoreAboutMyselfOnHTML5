Introduction

Hello stranger and welcome to my page! My name is Nestor Lee! I come from the small island state of Singapore! I am 23 years of age this year!


An old image of me in 2013 in Taiwan enjoying(?) a steak


Like many other Singaporean males, I served my National Service before entering University. In the army, I was an Automotive Technician, maintaining our countries fleet of Bronco ATTC as well as the Leopard 2 MBTs!



I ORD-ed on the 3rd of September 2014 and spent 1 year working for various companies such as Certis Cisco and RBS inside their IT departments getting some work related experience before finally securing a spot in a local university and starting my journey as a student again on the 7th of September 2015.

Why did I choose SIT?

Firstly, the main reason why I chose to study ICT is because I was exposed to computing since young. So naturally, I decided to study Computing after completing my O-Levels. After completing my diploma in Computer Engineering in SP, I enlisted into the army and in there, I decided to continue pursuing this field since I felt I had a passion for it.


SIT is first and only undergraduate degree program offered by a local autonomous university at that time that majors in information security. Without much doubt, I made up my mind to apply and try to enter SIT.




Secondly, I think it is also good that the school tries to instill the "SIT-DNA" on its students. Even if someone might be intellectually gifted, if he does not have a good upbringing, he might not become the most successful of a person.
